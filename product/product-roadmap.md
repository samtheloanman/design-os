# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Homepage & Core Infrastructure
Modernized homepage following production layout with hero, featured programs, credibility section, funded loans preview, testimonials, and territories module; establishes design system and primary CTA framework.

### 2. Structured Data & AI Content Strategy
JSON-LD schema markup for Loan Programs, Organizations, LocalBusiness, FAQs, Reviews, BreadcrumbList; semantic content tagging for entities (loan types, qualifications, rates, fees); content writer guidelines for AI-parseable copy (clarity, semantic keywords, structured formatting); ensures all content is citation-ready and discoverable by AI systems.

### 3. Loan Program Directory
All 179+ loan programs with consistent templates (Residential, Commercial, Hard Money, Non-QM, etc.), searchable/filterable directory, individual program pages with requirements, timelines, FAQs, and clear CTAs.

### 4. Service Locations
SEO-friendly pages for 50+ markets served, featuring local market summaries, relevant programs for that area, funded deals in that region, and strong internal linking.

### 5. Funded Loans & Social Proof
Modern gallery/showcase of funded deals with deal stories, images, filters (loan type, location, property type), plus testimonials and trust signals integrated throughout.

### 6. Partner Portal & Lead Management
Realtor/partner section with referral workflow, partner resources, lead submission integration, and callback management system.
